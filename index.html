About Personality

Hi, I’m Jeswin. I’m from Kerala, now in Bangalore, and I treat every project like a crafted artifact, not just another asset. I completed my B.Sc Computer Science at RVS College of Arts and Science in 2020 and have been building and designing experiences since then.


I explore music and DJing, and I enjoy editing video and photos in tools like Lightroom. I also experiment with chalk carving, feather painting and pencil sketches — all of which sharpen how I see composition, contrast and balance.


I love deep research, new tools and tracking tech trends. Staying updated daily keeps me excited and lets me translate ideas into concrete, business‑ready outputs for clients.
